How it works

An honest economy for your attention

1

Commit your quarters

Pick one to four quarter-hours and name what you'll get done. Longer commitments earn a bonus over chained short ones.

2

Focus, mint coins

A quiet timer counts each quarter. Finish a quarter, mint a coin. Check off a task, earn one more.

3

Spend deliberately

The rewards are ones you give yourself — and you set the prices. The vice you'd reach for anyway becomes something you've earned.

Streaks that pay

Show up daily and every coin you mint earns a growing bonus — half a percent per day, up to 5%. The longer you keep it alive, the richer each quarter gets.

Rewards you give yourself

Coffee at 20, gaming at 40, that handheld emulator at 950. You set the menu and the prices — then save up for the big stuff like you mean it.

Mac today, iPhone soon

A native Mac app with coins that fly, clink, and count. The same warm design is coming to iPhone and iPad — your data stays on your device.

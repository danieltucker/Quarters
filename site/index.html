How it works

An honest economy for your attention

1

Commit your quarters

Pick one to four quarter-hours and name what you'll get done. Longer commitments earn a bonus over chained short ones.

2

Focus, mint coins

A quiet timer counts each quarter. Finish a quarter, mint a coin. Check off a task, earn one more.

3

Spend deliberately

You set the rewards and the prices. The vice you'd reach for anyway becomes something you've earned.

Streaks that forgive

A daily streak rewards showing up — and one missed day bends it, never breaks it. The coach is warm, not cruel.

Your rewards, your prices

Coffee at 20, gaming at 40, that handheld emulator at 950. Save up for the big stuff like you mean it.

Lives in your menu bar

A compact companion window and a menu-bar timer. Your quarters follow you to iOS and the web next.
